EiC's C Specifications

EiC has been hand coded. Its parsing method is LL(N) and its grammar was derived from the LR grammar presented in Appendix A of [Kernighan and Ritchie, 1988]. Here the EiC programming language and specifications is given as this will, hopefully, allow for future developments. The syntax notation used when specifying the grammar for the EiC and C language is described in section § A.1, pg: .
